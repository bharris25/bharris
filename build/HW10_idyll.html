HW 10 using obesity percentages from Lake County, Illinois

By: Brandon Harris
Apr 26, 2022

Write up

For this assignment, I used data from the open data portal for my home county of Lake County, IL that shows obesity rates among the different cities in the county. My aim for the interactive features that I used is to allow the user to comb through the data in the table and see specific data points for whichever city that they select. I think that this is useful for speeding up the process of finding data for specific towns in Lake County and could help a user who would like to further explore this data find information easier.
